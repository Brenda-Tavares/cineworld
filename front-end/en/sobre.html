About CineWorld

CineWorld is a movie discovery website that helps you find the next film to watch.

Our Mission

To make searching for movies and series easier, providing an intuitive experience to discover new content.

What We Offer

  • Complete catalog of movies and series
  • Information about streaming platforms
  • AI-powered smart search
  • Favorites and history

Last updated: 2026

CineWorld does not host any video files. All content is provided by the official TMDB API (The Movie Database). Watch links are directed to legitimate streaming platforms (Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, etc.).
